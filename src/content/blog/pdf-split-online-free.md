PDF split karne ke do tarike hain:

1. **Specific pages extract karna** — Jaise 50 page ki PDF se page 5, 10, 15 nikalna
2. **Range se divide karna** — Jaise page 1-10 alag, 11-20 alag, 21-30 alag
