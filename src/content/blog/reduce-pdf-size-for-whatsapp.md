## Why Are PDFs Large?

Common reasons your PDF might be too big for WhatsApp:

- **Scanned documents** — Scans create image-heavy PDFs (5-50MB common)
- **High-res photos** — PDFs with embedded photos can be huge
- **Multiple pages** — Textbooks, reports, or multi-page documents
- **Embedded fonts** — Some PDFs include full font files
