# Image to PDF Convert Karna – Mobile aur Desktop Guide

Phone se li hui photo ko PDF banani hai? Scanned documents ki images ko proper PDF format mein convert karna hai? **Image to PDF convert** karna ab bahut easy hai — aur bilkul free.

## Kyun Images Ko PDF Mein Convert Karna Padta Hai?

Bahut saari situations mein aapko image se PDF banana padta hai:

- **Government forms** — ID proof photo ko PDF format mein chahiye
- **College assignments** — Handwritten notes ki photos ko PDF submission ke liye
- **Office documents** — Receipts aur invoices ki photos ko organize karne ke liye
- **WhatsApp documents** — Photos ko professional document format mein bhejne ke liye
- **Scan alternative** — Scanner nahi hai toh phone camera se photo le kar PDF banayein

## Image to PDF Kaise Convert Karein – Step by Step

### Step 1: Tool Open Karein

Apne browser mein yeh tools use kar sakte hain:
- [Image to PDF](/tools/image-to-pdf) — Any image format (JPG, PNG, JPEG, etc.)

"Convert to PDF" pe click karein. Aapki PDF ready hai download ke liye!

## Photo Se PDF Banane Ke Tips

1. **Achi lighting** mein photo lein — clear documents better convert hote hain
2. **Straight angle** se photo lein — tilted photos mein text padhna mushkil hota hai
3. **High resolution** rakhein — Blur photos se quality kharab PDF banti hai
4. **Crop karein** pehle — Extra background remove karein phone mein hi
5. **Size check karein** — Agar PDF badi ban rahi hai toh [compress](/tools/compress-pdf) karein
